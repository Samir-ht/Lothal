Our Heritage & Mission

Founded in 1978, the National Museum of Heritage and Culture stands as a beacon of historical preservation and cultural education. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and showcase the diverse cultural heritage that shapes our identity.

We are dedicated to providing immersive educational experiences that connect visitors with our shared history through interactive exhibits, educational programs, and community engagement.

Experience Our Museum

Our Mission

The National Museum of Heritage and Culture is dedicated to the collection, preservation, study, and exhibition of works of art and cultural artifacts that represent the diverse heritage of our nation and the world.

Our mission is to:

  • Preserve cultural heritage for future generations
  • Provide educational opportunities for visitors of all ages
  • Foster understanding and appreciation of diverse cultures
  • Support research and scholarship in art, history, and culture
  • Engage with communities to promote cultural awareness

Through our exhibitions, educational programs, and community outreach, we strive to inspire curiosity, creativity, and a deeper understanding of our shared human experience.

Our History

The National Museum of Heritage and Culture was established in 1978 with a vision to create a space where the rich cultural heritage of our nation could be preserved and celebrated.

Key Milestones:

  • 1978: Foundation of the museum with an initial collection of 500 artifacts
  • 1985: Expansion of the main building to include dedicated exhibition spaces
  • 1992: Launch of the educational outreach program
  • 2003: Major renovation and modernization of facilities
  • 2010: Opening of the new wing dedicated to contemporary cultural expressions
  • 2018: Celebration of 40 years with the launch of digital archives
  • 2022: Completion of state-of-the-art conservation laboratory

Today, the museum houses over 50,000 artifacts and welcomes more than 500,000 visitors annually, continuing its legacy as a center for cultural preservation and education.

Maritime Heritage Collection

Our Maritime Heritage Collection showcases the rich history of seafaring traditions, naval architecture, and maritime exploration that have shaped our cultural identity.

This exhibit features over 200 artifacts including ship models, navigational instruments, maritime paintings, and interactive displays that bring the seafaring experience to life.

Exhibit Highlights:

  • • 18th-century navigation instruments
  • • Scale models of historic vessels
  • • Interactive navigation simulator
  • • Maritime art collection
